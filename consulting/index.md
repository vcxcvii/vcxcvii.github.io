## Short version

I work with founders and marketing leaders who have enough strategy decks and not enough finished work.

Sometimes the positioning is muddy. Sometimes the launch exists as twelve disconnected documents. Sometimes the team is busy, smart, and still unable to move one important GTM priority across the finish line.

That is the work.

[Book 30 minutes on my calendar](https://cal.com/varun-choraria/30min) if you already know something is stuck.

## Problems worth calling me about

- Marketing still lives inside the founder's head.
- The strategy is clear, but capacity is not.
- Product marketing is shipping feature lists instead of a point of view.
- Sales, marketing, and product are telling three versions of the same story.
- AI has increased output and made the operating system worse.
- Nobody owns the work between the plan and the pipeline.

I do not arrive with a generic transformation framework. Every company is broken in its own way. The job is to understand where the handoffs fail, decide what matters, and ship the fix.

## How I work

I work directly with the people responsible for the number. No junior handoff. No strategy deck thrown over a wall.

First, we diagnose the real constraint. Then we agree on what finished means. Then we build.

AI sits underneath the work, not on top of the headline. Agents help with research, first drafts, analysis, documentation, QA, and repetitive operations. Judgment, taste, and accountability stay human.

Most useful engagements end with something live, documented, and easier for your team to operate without me.

## What I am not selling

- AI workshops built around tool demos
- Guaranteed pipeline numbers
- Fifty pieces of content because a model can produce them
- A prettier strategy deck with no owner
- Permanent dependence on a consultant

If you need somebody to agree with every idea, I am the wrong person. If you want a senior operator who will question the premise and still help ship the answer, we should talk.
